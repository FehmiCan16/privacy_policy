Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how OneForge Games ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our mobile game Ticket to Castle (the "App").

By installing or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not require you to register, sign in, or provide any personal information to play the App. We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or any other personally identifying information directly.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, certain information is collected automatically by us or by third-party services:

  • Device information: device model, operating system version, language, country/region, screen resolution.
  • Advertising identifier: Google Advertising ID (AAID), used for personalized advertising.
  • App usage data: levels played, in-game progress, session duration, crash reports.
  • Approximate location: derived from IP address (country/region level only — not GPS).

1.3 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Your game progress (tickets collected, mission progress, audio preferences) is saved locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect information about you:

2.1 Google AdMob (advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process information including your advertising identifier and device information to show relevant ads.

  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Personalized ads can be controlled via your device settings.

2.2 Unity LevelPlay (ad mediation)

We use Unity LevelPlay to manage and serve advertisements through multiple ad networks.

  • Privacy Policy: https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

2.3 Unity Ads

Unity Ads is one of the ad networks served via mediation.

  • Privacy Policy: https://unity.com/legal/game-player-and-app-user-privacy-policy

2.4 Google Play Services

The App relies on Google Play Services for core functionality.

  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. How Information Is Used

  • To display advertisements (including rewarded video and banner ads)
  • To measure ad performance and prevent fraudulent activity
  • To diagnose crashes and improve App stability
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Advertising and Rewarded Ads

The App shows banner advertisements and offers optional rewarded video advertisements. Rewarded ads are shown only when you explicitly choose to watch one (e.g., to receive in-game bonus time or a revive). You can play the App without watching rewarded ads at any time.

To opt out of personalized advertising:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > "Opt out of Ads Personalization"
  • You can also reset your Advertising ID at any time from the same screen.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us using the information below.

For users in the European Union, the age threshold may be 16, depending on local law.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the limited information collected through the App. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Game save data is stored only on your device.

7. Data Retention

We do not retain personal information on our servers, as we do not collect any directly. Third-party services (AdMob, Unity Ads) retain data according to their own policies. Game save data remains on your device until you uninstall the App.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • The right to access information that has been collected about you
  • The right to request deletion of personal information
  • The right to opt out of personalized advertising
  • The right to lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise these rights regarding our App, contact us using the email below. For data held by third parties, please reach out to them directly.

9. International Data Transfers

Information collected by third-party services may be processed in countries outside your own, including the United States. By using the App, you consent to such transfers as permitted by applicable law.
